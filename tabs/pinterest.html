Feedback & Suggestions for Pinterest’s Mood Board Experience

A mood board is a fantastic starting point for planning any campaign, experimenting with new looks, refreshing personal style, or simply scrolling through endless pins to gather ideas.

While organizing my board, I noticed some limitations that could be improved: currently, pins can only be moved one at a time, and there isn’t a zoom-out or overview feature to give users a better sense of the overall layout.

Adding a side panel with tools to create collages, drag multiple pins simultaneously, and view suggested pins while organizing would greatly enhance the user experience. This would make it easier for users to curate boards with a strong visual flow, keep them organized, and continue discovering inspiration seamlessly.
